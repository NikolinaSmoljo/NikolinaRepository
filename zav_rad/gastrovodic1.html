Gastro suggestions

When it comes to delicacies, has Zagora for an answer; virgin nature, resourceful people who prepare food and layers of tradition, the tables are laid intoxicating dishes, full of taste and smell. A very old way of preparing bread and meat and vegetables on the hearth - baked, an iron lid or metal pan, clay pots or cast iron.

Offer off Dalmatian hinterland: many restaurants, resorts, pizza place, fast food, and more. They offer local specialties or other food crops.
